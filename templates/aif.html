Understanding Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs)

For investors who’ve mastered the basics, true diversification begins beyond the public markets. At Peacock Wealth Management, our AIF solutions offer access to sophisticated investment opportunities that combine innovation, strategy, and institutional-level precision.

Through due diligence, manager evaluation, and thematic research, we ensure that every AIF allocation complements your equity, debt, and global exposure — strengthening your overall wealth structure.

Our Philosophy

We believe alternative investments aren’t about complexity — they’re about clarity in opportunity. Our advisory team handpicks funds and strategies that are transparent, compliant, and aligned with your broader portfolio vision.

Ideal for investors seeking diversification, long-term capital appreciation, and access to opportunities traditionally available only to institutions.
