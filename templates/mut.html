What Is a Mutual Fund?

A Mutual Fund pools money from multiple investors and invests in equities, bonds & other assets through expert fund managers.

How It Works

  • Professional Fund Manager invests your money
  • Diversified across companies & sectors
  • NAV determines investor returns

Why Choose It

  • Less risk vs single stock investing
  • Simple, flexible, and transparent
  • Suitable for all investor types

Types of Mutual Funds
